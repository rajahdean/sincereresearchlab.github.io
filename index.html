Introduction

sincereResearchLab is a dot com start up research resource of choice, created by Zimbabweans for Zimbabweans and to serve the Regional and Global Communities. We hande all your data analysis needs, whether you are an individual or a corporate entity. We strive to serve your needs better.Our vision is to be a unique, research oriented, pace-setting and stakeholder-driven Research Centre that will support your academic research through a personalized delivery of seamless services.

Our Mission

Guided by our Vision-, sincereResearchLAB aims at being a leading internationally respected Research Centre that is committed to:

  • providing competitive business intelligence for decision making through quality research that will support information mining using a multiplicity of web content mining and web structural mining tools;
  • supporting organisations in penetrating new markets through market research;
  • a sustainable and continuous training regime for academic researchers (both lecturers and students) by equipping them with data analytical skills using statistical applications such as SPSS and R Programming.
  • human capital development in the area of academic research through training workshops and conferences;
  • the promotion of Information Communication Technologies (ICTs) in the delivery of quality and relevant research ;
  • bridging the digital divide between business and computing, and rural communities and urbanites in through ICT enabled research;
  • strict confidentiality between the Research Centre and its clientele, underpinned on good corporate governance policies and practices.

About our company

We a a start up dot com consultancy located in Gweru Zimbabwe. Our primary focus is to provide you with assistance in analysing your research data, whether you are an academic or non-academic researcher. This will be carried out at a prescribed fee (chargeable in United States Dollars for foreign based clients or in Bond Note equivalent for locally based clients). Our rates are project related and may be discussed with the Senior Researcher, Dino Rajah (Email: rajah.dino@gmail.com). Therefore the cost is guided by the scope of work to be undertaken. It should be noted that we do not write dissertations for clients, nor do we carryout a write up of the presentation and analysis. Work on the client's file may commence upon payment of a registration fee of US$20.00. This amount is not refundable. Delivery of the completed work will be made based upon payment of the full fee that has been agreed upon between sincereResearchLab and the client. The lead time for completion of work will also be determined in the work contract.

PAYMENT MAY BE MADE VIA ECOCASH OR THROUGH A DIRECT TRANSFER INTO OUR ZIMBABWE BANK ACCOUNT. NO REFUNDS WILL BE ENTERTAINED.
